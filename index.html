Paper Presentation

Pitch your brilliant ideas, impress the jury, and prove that your research can rule the robotics world.

Cadscape

Put your engineering skills to the test in the SolidWorks Design Challenge! This competition invites students to showcase their creativity and technical expertise using SolidWorks, the industry-leading 3D CAD software. Participants will design and model innovative solutions to a given problem statement, competing for the title of the best designer.

Code Nascar

Speed meets syntax! Race through code checkpoints and reach the finish line—bug free (hopefully).CodeNascar is an exhilarating coding competition that puts your problem-solving and algorithmic thinking to the ultimate test. Designed for speed and precision, this event challenges participants to push their programming skills into high gear.

Mind Quest

Mind Quest is the ultimate test of logic, creativity, and quick thinking. Dive into a series of intense mental challenges that push your problem-solving skills to the edge. Across three thrilling rounds, teams will face puzzles, rapid-fire tasks, and strategic thinking games that demand sharp minds and smooth coordination.

Verbal Warfare

Drop the mic — or don’t. Debate your heart out and let your arguments do the punching.

Bidding Wars

Brains meet bidding. Spend your (fake) bucks wisely and win the most prized picks! Step into the world of global dynamics with Bidding Wars, a quiz that challenges your knowledge of international economies, industries, and resource distribution. Participants will tackle a diverse range of questions covering economic indicators, trade patterns, natural resource hotspots, and country-specific industrial strengths.

Connecting Dots

Connecting Dots is a thrilling and comically suspenseful event where participants become detectives for a day. Step into a world of mystery as you analyze tricky clues, question suspicious characters, and piece together the puzzle to solve the ultimate whodunit. It’s a fun mix of logic, laughter, and light-hearted drama—perfect for anyone who loves a good mystery with a twist of humor!

Tech Odyssey

AGet ready for the ultimate treasure hunt – where adventure meets technology! Step into a world filled with cryptic codes, digital riddles, and hidden secrets waiting to be uncovered. Navigate through tricky clues, solve mind-bending puzzles, and race against the clock as you journey through a maze of mystery and excitement.

Soccer Bot

Soccer Bot is a high-energy competitive event where teams face off in a league-style tournament using remote-controlled robots to play soccer.

Sky Surge

SkySurge is a cutting-edge simulation competition designed to test and enhance participants' piloting skills in a virtual environment. By incorporating physics-based simulation mechanics, the event challenges pilots to demonstrate precision, control, and strategic flight maneuvers.

Mr./Ms. Botsha

Mr/Ms.Botsha is an exciting and competitive event where participants must demonstrate their creativity, spontaneity, and performance skills across 3 to 4 challenging rounds such as spin a yarn, channel surfing, shipwreck. Expect where you'll need to impress both the judges and audience with your speaking, acting and dramatic abilities. To win, you must deliver a standout performance that sets you apart from the competition!
